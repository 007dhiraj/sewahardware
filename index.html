HARDWARE

Furniture hardware is the functional metal
parts and fittings that make furniture work.
These include sliding rails, hinges, sofa legs,
lifters, backrests, springs, gun nails, foot codes, connections, baskets, and decorations

LAMINATES

Laminate is a surfacing material made of stacked kraft paper sheets covered in thermosetting resin. When heat is applied, the resin hardens. Laminate sheets are often used to cover wooden furniture to give it a more attractive look. Laminate is also used to surface cabinets, ceilings, walls, and external facades. Laminate sheets are usually 8x4 feet and range in thickness from 0.6 mm to 1.5 mm

PLYWOOD

Plywood is a composite material manufactured from thin layers,
or "plies", of wood veneer that are glued together with adjacent
layers, having both glued with each other at right angle or at
90 degrees angle.
